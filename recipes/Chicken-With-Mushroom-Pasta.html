Chicken with Mushroom Pasta

Chicken Pasta Recipe

This easy one pot chicken pasta recipe will be a quick weeknight go to meal. Chicken and penne noodles covered in a creamy mushroom sauce and topped with shredded parmesan cheese

Ingredients

    2 16 ounce cans white meat chicken
    2 8 ounce cans Campbells concentrated Mushroom Soup
    1 18 ounce box of Penne noodles
    16 ounces of milk
    1/2 tsp garlic powder
    1/2 tsp onion powder
    1/2 tsp paprika
    1/2 tsp sage, thyme or rosemary
    2 tbsp dried parsley
    Salt and pepper to taste
    Shredded parmesan cheese to taste

Steps

    1. In a large pasta pot bring to boil 8 cups of water
    2. Add penne pasta. Cook to desired tenderness
    3. Drain pasta and set aside
    4. In same pot combine the 2 cans of mushroom soup and milk. Whisk until blended
    5. Bring soup mixture to a slow boil
    6. Add spices and mix well
    7. Drain broth from the canned chicken but do not rinse
    8. Add chicken to the soup mixture and stir until chicken is evenly distributed in the sauce
    9. Bring back up to slow boil
<01>10. Add back in the noodles stirring until noodles are thoroughly coated
    11. Heat mixture stirring often
    12. Serve warm and refrigerate leftovers
