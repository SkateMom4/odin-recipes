Sheperds Pie with Ground Chicken

Ingredients

A new healthier take on traditional Shepherds Pie using ground chicken instead of ground beef. All the flavor and none of the grease!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground 85% lean chicken
  • 5 russet potatoes
  • 3 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbs parsley flakes
  • 1 tbsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp ground thyme
  • 2 tsp salt divided
  • 2 tsp pepper divided

Directions

  1. 1. Cut the potatoes into 2" chunks. You can peel them or leave the peel on
  2. 2. Bring 5 cups of water to a roiling bowl. Place cut potatoes into pot and cover stirring often. Remove when they are fork tender
  3. 3. In a large frying pan cook the brown chicken until there is no pink visible. Use the 85% lean chicken to add flavor and moisture to the dish
  4. 4. In the frying pan add the onion, garlic salt, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, thyme, parsley and 1 tsp each of the salt and pepper to the ground chicken.
  5. 5. Mash the boiled potatoes adding the remaining salt and pepper. Mash them into a smooth but stiff consistency. The potatoes will make a peak when done.
  6. 6. In a 4 quart ceramic or glass baking dish layer the ground chicken, then the cheese and finally the potatoes
  7. Place the baking dish in a 350 degree oven for 25 minutes covered. Remove the cover in the last 5 minutes to lightly brown the potatoes
  8. Remove from oven and serve. Refrigerate any leftovers
